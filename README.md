### 🌱 About me

- 🎓 Studying **Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge** at the University of Bologna, originally from Marche, Italy.
- 🗺️ Working with **cultural data, Linked Open Data, interactive maps, and digital storytelling**
- 🏳️‍🌈 Contributed to projects on **Italian LGBTQIA+ history** and Bologna's cultural heritage
- 🎮 Into all **geek and nerd stuff, but in a cool way I swear...**
- ✍️ Also write for a music magazines and work in event-organizing here in Bologna, Italy.

### 🚀 Featured projects

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%">

**🎬 [Lima Live Museum of Movie Locations](https://alicesgarlata.github.io/limalivecinemamuseum)**

Interactive virtual museum mapping 22 filming locations in Lima, Peru, across 19 films (1938–2024). Built with Leaflet.js and Bootstrap, with content that adapts by tone and length.

`Leaflet.js` `JavaScript` `Bootstrap` `GitHub Pages`

</td>
<td width="50%">

**🏳️‍🌈 Fighting for Pride — The March Through Time**

Immersive Twine prototype narrating the history of Pride in Bologna as a continuous march through eight historical eras, built with the Cassero LGBTI+ Center.

`Twine` `Harlowe` `CSS` `Storytelling`

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%">

**⚔️ God of War — LOD Project**

Linked data modeling (TEI/XML → RDF) of the *God of War* (2018) Wikipedia page: entities, myth/fiction relations, and a custom `gow:reinterprets` property.

`TEI/XML` `RDF` `Python` `rdflib` `SPARQL`

</td>
<td width="50%">

**📚 Citation Management System**

Bibliographic citation management system with a dual database setup (Blazegraph/SPARQL + SQLite), built as a team project for Computational Management of Data.

`Python` `Blazegraph` `SPARQL` `SQLite`
